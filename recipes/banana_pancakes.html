Banana Pancakes

These are our favorite banana pancakes. Simple and easy with no added sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinamon
  • Dash of salt
  • 3/4 cup ripe banana (1.5 bananas)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Medium bowl, whisk together all the dry ingredients - the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the wet ingredients - smashed bananas, egg, milk.
  3. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients to combine as one.
  4. Heat a large nonstick pan over medium heat. Melt butter.
  5. Using measuring cup, scoop and pour batter into pan. Cook pancakes about 3-4 minutes until bubbling. Then flip and cook another 1-2 minutes.
