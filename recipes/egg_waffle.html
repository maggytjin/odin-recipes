Egg Waffles

This is such a quick and easy breakfast, the whole family will love!

It includes everything delicious - onion, garlic, and egg of course.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • Garlic powder
  • Diced onion
  • Shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs together.
  2. Add all the remaining ingredients into the bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture into a waffle iron and let it sit for 4-5 minutes.
