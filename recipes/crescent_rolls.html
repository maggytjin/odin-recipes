Stuffed Crescent Rolls

Elevate the Crescent Rolls to something so much better. Add some extra cheese and some delicious meat to the dish to make it a full meal. This is a great dish to make with leftover meat.

You can customize these to your own likings. If you want just cheese. Maybe you want just the meat? Whatever you want, make it your way.

Ingredients

  • Crescent Rolls
  • Cream cheese
  • Shredded cheese
  • Shredded chicken or beef

Instructions

  1. Roll out the Crescent Rolls.
  2. Spread a layer of cream cheese onto the Crescent Rolls.
  3. Sprinkle on the shredded meat.
  4. Roll the Crescent Rolls.
  5. Turn on the oven to 350 degrees and bake for 10 minutes.
