Banana Oatmeal Muffins

Quick and easy breakfast or snack.

Makes: 12 muffins

Ingredients

  • 4 servings of hash brown
  • Chopped veggies of your choice - mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, etc.
  • 3 TBSP Mayonnaise
  • 3 TBSP Sour cream
  • 1 cup Shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Spray muffin tin with oil.
  3. Mix all ingredients together.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
