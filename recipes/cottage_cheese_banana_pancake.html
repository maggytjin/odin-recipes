Cottage Cheese Banana Pancakes

So simple to make. Just toss everything into a blender and flip on the grill. I need to perfect my flipping because I make a mess each time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 medium banana
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Throw everything into a blender and blend until completely smooth.
  2. Turn burner on medium low. Melt some butter and pour on batter.
  3. Flip when the pancakes start to bubble on top. Cook for another minute.
  4. Top with blueberries, chocolate, or peanut butter! Whatever your heart desires.
