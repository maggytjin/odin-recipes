Baked Brussels Sprouts

These are one of our favorite brussel sprout recipes I've found. We gobble it all up in one sitting.

Ingredients

  • Brussels sprouts
  • Honey
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut ends of brussels sprouts off and in half.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  3. Set the oven to 400 degrees and bake for 12 minutes.
  4. Flip the brussels sprouts and throw it back in the oven for another 10 minutes.
  5. Take the brussels sprouts out and throw them into a bowl. Mix in the honey and balsamic vinegar. Mix it all together.
  6. Serve warm.
