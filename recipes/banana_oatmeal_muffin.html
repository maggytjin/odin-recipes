Banana Oatmeal Muffins

Quick and easy breakfast or snack.

Makes: 12 muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 bananas
  • 6 oz of yogurt or milk
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Blend rolled oats together for 30 seconds to make oat flour.
  3. Add in the rest of the ingredients (except chocolate chips) and blend.
  4. Spray muffin tin with oil and then pour mixture into muffin tins.
  5. Add chocolate chips onto each muffin.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes. Let cool in pan for 5-10 minutes and then remove to cooling rack.
